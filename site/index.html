Questions

The details people usually ask about.

If your setup is unusual, the Enterprise plan can cover custom hosting and domains.

How is this different from a general chat tool?

Lead Qualifier is narrower by design. It checks each visitor against your ideal customer profile, scores the conversation, and routes the result. It is for lead screening, not a full customer support suite.

What happens when the assistant gets a verdict wrong?

You can change any verdict from the dashboard and add a note explaining why. The assistant uses those corrections as context in future conversations.

Can it invent prices or make promises?

The assistant is instructed not to invent business details. If it does not have an answer, it tells the visitor that your team will follow up and flags the conversation for review.

Will it work with my website?

If you can add a script tag, it will work. That includes WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, and custom websites. You can control which pages show the widget.

What can I do with leads that are not a fit?

You can end the conversation politely or invite the visitor to join your newsletter. The transcript remains in your dashboard either way.

Can an agency offer this under its own brand?

Yes. The Agency plan includes 10 client accounts and API management. White label options remove Lead Qualifier branding from the widget.
