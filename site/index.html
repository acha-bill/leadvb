What it does

A junior sales rep that never sleeps, never forgets a question.

Qualification built in — not bolted on

Every conversation is scored 0–100 against your ideal customer profile across budget, authority, need and timeline. You set the bar; the assistant collects names, emails and phone numbers naturally along the way. Unqualified visitors are let down politely — or pointed to your newsletter.

Proactive triggers

Open after a delay, on exit intent, or only on specific pages like /pricing. Capture the visitors who'd never click first.

It learns from you

Overrode a verdict? Your correction is fed back into future conversations. The assistant gets sharper every week.

Human handoff

Visitor wants a person? You're pinged instantly and can jump into the live chat from your dashboard.

No CRM lock-in

Email, Slack, or signed webhooks to any system. Your leads, your data, your stack.

See the time you're saving

Leads qualified vs. screened out, average time to verdict, AI confidence, and estimated hours of manual screening you didn't do. Plus a weekly report in your inbox — proof the thing pays for itself.

Bilingual by default

English, French, Spanish — or auto-detect and match the visitor. Built for Canadian and US businesses.

White-label for agencies

Resell it to your clients under your own brand. Manage every client account from one place via the API.

FAQ

Fair questions.

How is this different from Intercom or Drift?

Those are enterprise support-and-sales suites — powerful, complex, and priced like it (Drift starts around $2,500/mo). Lead Qualifier does one job: qualify your website visitors against your ideal customer profile and hand you only the good ones. Plain-English setup, flat pricing, live in 14 minutes.

What if the AI gets a verdict wrong?

Every conversation is visible in your dashboard, and you can override any verdict with one click. Your override — and the note you attach — is fed back into future conversations, so the assistant learns your judgment over time.

Will it make things up about my business?

The assistant is instructed to never invent facts, prices or promises. When it doesn't know something, it says the team will follow up — and flags the conversation for you.

Does it work on WordPress / Webflow / Squarespace?

Yes — it's a single script tag, so it works anywhere you can paste HTML. On WordPress, any "insert headers & footers" plugin does it in one minute.

What happens to visitors who aren't a fit?

Your choice: the assistant wraps up politely, or invites them to your newsletter so the visit still builds your audience. Either way you see the transcript and can overrule.

Can my agency resell this?

Yes. The Agency plan gives you 10 client accounts you can manage via API and price however you like; the white-label tier removes our branding entirely.
